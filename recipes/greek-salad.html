Greek Salad

Description

My favorite side dish. Zesty, tangy, crunchy, salty, magnificent.

Ingredients

  • 500g tomato
  • 500g cucumber
  • 100g onion
  • 200g olive
  • 300g feta
  • 1 tsp oregano

Steps

  1. Chop the tomato and cucumber into little cubes
  2. Mix everything until feta blends with the vegetable juices
